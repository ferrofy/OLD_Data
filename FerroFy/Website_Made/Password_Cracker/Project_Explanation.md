## 🚀 How To Use

1. **Enter Password**: Type A Short Target Password In The Input Box (E.g., `A`, `1`, `A1`, `B!`).
2. **Initiate Crack**: Click The "Initiate Crack" Button.
3. **Watch Output**: Observe The Algorithm Attempting Random/Sequential Combinations While Tracking The Time Elapsed.
4. **Result**: Once The Exact Match Is Found, The System Stops And Shows A Red Alert!

> ⚠️ *Note: Due To The Exponential Nature Of Bruteforcing, Long Passwords Will Take A Substantial Amount Of Time To Crack! Keep It Short For Quick Testing.*
